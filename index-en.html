Who Are We?

DYMIEC S.A. de C.V. (Distribution and Maintenance for the Entertainment and Construction Industry) is a Mexican company based in Mexico City.

We have extensive experience in logistics, technical support, and rental of specialized equipment for concerts, construction, stage structures, material distribution, among many other services.

Our commitment is to provide quality, safety, and efficiency in every project.
